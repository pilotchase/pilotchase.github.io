Sick Removal

Process:
  1. Modify sequence with 25/SK or 25/US and remove balance
  2. GOA to Base (A3D) or Home (A12)
  3. Send Contact Slip
  4. Recover open legs
FOS Entries:
                                        HE/SEQ/DD/25/SK ¶
                                        H6/FLT/DD/STA ¶
                                        H6/FLT/DD/STA »
                                        
                                        HZ/POS »
                                        ET »

                                        GOA/EMP/FLT/DD/FA/STA/STA/TKT »
                                        ET »
Notes:
  1. If FA wants to be removed from a future sequence, transfer to FASKD.

Fatigue (Origination)

Process (LH):
  1. Establish Fit For Duty Time
  2. FEM Tool
  3. Remove pilot from the sequence with 2G/FT
  4. Build RPT seq with difference between current time and SI time
  5. Attach RPT seq to the pilot
  6. Add planned absense with FT code
  7. Kill original seq
  8. Recover open flying
FOS Entries:
                                    2G/EMP/SEQ/DD/FT »
                                    ET »
                                    
                                    H4(D/I)/BASE/EQ//DD ¶
                                    H7/RPT/DD/STA/STA/SITIME/CURR TIME - SI TIME (x.xx) »
                                    HZ/POS »
                                    ET »

                                    HU/EMP/POS/SEQ/DD/RP »
                                    ET »
                                    
                                    A4/EMP/FT/DDMMMYY/DDMMMYY//STARTTIME/ENDTIME »
                                    ET »

                                    //Kill orig seq
                                    HK/SEQ/DD/POS »
                                    ET »
Process (RSV):
  1. Establish Fit For Duty Time
  2. FEM Tool
  3. Remove pilot from the sequence with 2G/FT
  4. Build RPT seq with difference between current time and SI time
  5. NBA RPT seq to the pilot
  6. Assign callout pay with HM entry
  7. Add planned absense with FT code
  8. Kill original seq
  9. Transfer to PISKD to assign RAP
  10. Recover open flying

                                    ET »

                                    HZ/POS »
                                    ET »

                                    NBA/EMP/SEQ/DD/POS/RP »
                                    ET »

                                    //EQ is the 2 digit EQ code of first leg after FTG
                                    HM/EMP/DD/138/EQ/2.00 »
                                    ET »

                                    A4/EMP/FT/DDMMMYY/DDMMMYY//STARTTIME/ENDTIME »
                                    ET »

                                    HK/SEQ/DD/POS »

Fatigue (Mid-Sequence)

  1. Establish Fit For Duty Time
  2. FEM Tool
  3. Modify sequence with 25/MI, add FTG leg, add RPT leg 0.01 after FTG leg, remove affected legs
  4. If in base, add planned absense with FT code
  5. If RSV in base, transfer to PISKD for RAP
  6. Recover open flying
FOS Entries:
                                HE/SEQ/DD/25/MI ¶
                                H9/FTG/DD/STA/STA/TIME/0.00 ¶
                                H7/RPT/DD/STA/STA/TIME/0.00 ¶
                                H6/FLT/DD/STA »
                                HZ/POS »
                                ET »
                                
                                A4/EMP/FT/DDMMMYY/DDMMMYY//STARTTIME/ENDTIME »
                                ET »

  4. Send contact slip

Sick Removal (RSV)

Process:
  1. Enter absense in FOS with code SK, with a start date of today and tentative clear on last day of sequence
  2. Modify sequence with 25/15 and remove balance, and add SUR back to base
  3. Set sick
  4. Unset sick if flown same day
  5. GOA pilot to base (A3D) or home (A12)
  6. Send contact slip
  7. Recover open legs

                                ET »

                                //If HAS flown same day
                                GC/EMP/DDMMM//U/SK »
                                ET »

                                GOA/EMP/FLT/DD/FA/STA/STA/TKT »

Delay Until Next Day (Origination)

Process:
  1. Remove from entire seq with 2G/17
  2. Kill original seq
  3. Rebuild seq for day the flight is now departing and send to PISKD
  4. Build RPT seq and send to PISKD via Ctrl panel with NSR
FOS Entries:
                        2G/EMP/SEQ/DD/17 »
                        ET »
                        
                        HK/SEQ/DD/POS »
                        ET »

Delay Until Next Day (Mid)

Process:
  1. Modify seq with RO/17 (RFW), AF/AF (w/o RFW), 25/17 (RSV)
  2. End FDP with report leg
  3. Send override request via control panel
  4. Set new departure time
  5. Recover open flights
FOS Entries:
                        HE/SEQ/DD/ADD/RMV ¶
                        H7/RPT/DD/STA/STA/TIME/0.00 »
                        HZ/POS »
